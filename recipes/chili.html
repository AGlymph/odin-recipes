Chili

Description

Slow cooked chili with ground beef, veggies, and generous helping of spices. Perfect for a fall day.

Ingredients

  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1 chopped white onion
  • 2tbsp chopped garlic
  • 45oz, about 3 cans, of your favorite beans varieties
  • 30oz of crushed tomatoes/li>
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 3tbsp chili powder
  • 1tbsp garlic powder
  • 1tbsp ground cumin
  • 1tbsp lemon juice
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot on medium heat add the oil and warm
  2. Add the onion with a pinch of salt, cook until fragrant
  3. Add the beef, cook until brown
  4. Add the other veggies, beans, and spices
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes. Bring to a low boil
  6. Drop the temp to low and let simmer for 30 minutes to and hour
  7. Finish off with lemon juice, salt, and pepper
