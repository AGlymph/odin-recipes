Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The most famous of all sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of favorite bread
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1/2 tablespoon of favorite jelly or jam

Steps

  1. Put one slice of bread on a plate
  2. Using a knife spread peanut butter on the bread
  3. Put other slice of bread on the plate next to the first slice
  4. Using a knife spread jelly on the second piece of bread
  5. Lift the peanut butter covered bread, turn over, and put on the jelly bread
